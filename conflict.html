Faces of hunger & conflict

Where there is conflict, there is hunger, and where there is hunger, there is often conflict


Hunger and conflict are interconnected, without peace we will never achieve zero hunger. Breaking the links between conflict and hunger can advance both food security and peace.
The two-way links between hunger and conflict are well established. violent conflict is destructive to virtually every aspect of a food system, from production, harvesting, processing, and transport to input supply, financing, marketing, and heightened food insecurity can contribute to violent conflict.
Without resolving food insecurity.it is difficult to build sustainable peace, and without peace the likelihood of ending global hunger is minimal.

"We can’t end hunger if we don’t end conflict"
